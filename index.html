About


Hi! I am a San Francisco Bay Area native through and through. I've spent the majority of my professional life in thehealthcare industry, which has taken me on a journey. I started as a Business Application Coordinator, where I supported the front-end users and worked closely with the application development team and support staff. From there, I became an end-user/ support staff while I went to college. I later moved on to solution development and technical support. These experiences have given me a unique perspective and have opened my eyes and redirected me to becoming a softwaredeveloper and stepping outside of healthcare.

My current interest includes increasing my knowledge of Javascript and Visual Design, seeing where I can take it, there is nothing like taking a thought and making it into something.
